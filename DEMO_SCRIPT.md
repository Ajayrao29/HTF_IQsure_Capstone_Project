# 🎬 IQsure Full Project Demo Script & Walkthrough

This guide provides a step-by-step flow for a professional video demo of the **IQsure** platform. 

## 📌 Preparation Tips
- **Reset Data**: Clear your browser cache or restart the backend to ensure a "clean" start (unless you want to show existing data).
- **Screen Resolution**: Record at 1080p for clarity.
- **Audio**: Use a good microphone and record in a quiet environment.
- **Pacing**: Move clearly but keep it snappy. Use hover effects to show responsiveness.

---

## 🕒 Video Flow (Estimated: 12-15 minutes)

### Part 1: Introduction (0:00 - 1:00)
- **Visuals**: Start on the Landing Page.
- **What to say**: 
    > "Welcome to **IQsure**, a modern, gamified insurance literacy platform. We built this platform with a clear mission: to make insurance education engaging, rewarding, and accessible. IQsure isn't just a policy management tool; it's an ecosystem where users learn about insurance, earn rewards through gamified quizzes, and unlock real premium discounts based on their knowledge."

### Part 2: The Customer Journey (1:00 - 6:00)

#### 1. Registration & Landing (1 min)
- **Action**: Navigate to Landing page -> Register -> Login.
- **What to say**: 
    > "The journey begins on our clean, responsive landing page, styled with professional Hartford branding. Let's register a new user. Our registration process is swift, and once logged in, the user is greeted by their personalized dashboard."

#### 2. Learning & Quizzes (2 mins)
- **Action**: Go to **Learning Center** -> Select a **Quiz** (e.g., Health Insurance Basics) -> Start Quiz.
- **What to say**: 
    > "The heart of IQsure is the Learning Center. Here, users can browse educational content before testing their knowledge. Let's take the 'Health Insurance Basics' quiz. Notice the interactive UI, the timer, and the progress tracker. Correct answers earn points, and finishing quickly gives a speed bonus—which directly impacts policy discounts later!"

#### 3. Achievements & Leaderboard (1 min)
- **Action**: Finish quiz -> Go to **Badges/Achievements** -> **Leaderboard**.
- **What to say**: 
    > "After finishing a quiz, users immediately see their results. They earn badges like 'Quiz Beginner' or 'Knowledge Pro'. This gamification keeps users engaged. We can also see the global Leaderboard where top learners are celebrated."

#### 4. Policy Purchase & Smart Discounts (2 mins)
- **Action**: Go to **Policies** -> Click **Preview My Premium** on a policy.
- **What to say**: 
    > "Now for the most innovative part. When a user looks at an insurance policy, they don't just see a price. They see a **Premium Breakdown**. Because I scored well on the quiz and earned badges, the system applied dynamic discount rules. I'm saving 25% because I'm a 'Knowledge Pro'! This bridges the gap between learning and financial benefit."
- **Action**: Click **Buy Policy** -> Show the application form (Nominees, Family members).

---

### Part 3: The Underwriter Pipeline (6:00 - 8:30)

- **Action**: Logout -> Login as **Underwriter** (or show the Underwriter Dashboard).
- **What to say**: 
    > "Let's switch roles to the **Underwriter**. The Underwriter Dashboard provides a birds-eye view of policy applications, total risk, and pending tasks. As an underwriter, I can see the policy we just applied for."
- **Action**: Go to **Pending Policies** -> Open the application.
- **What to say**: 
    > "Here, I can review every detail: the applicant's profile, their family members, and any pre-existing conditions. This holistic view allows for informed decision-making. I'll go ahead and approve this policy."

---

### Part 4: Claims Officer Workspace (8:30 - 10:30)

- **Action**: Logout -> Login as **Customer** -> Go to **My Policies** -> **File a Claim**.
- **What to say**: 
    > "Back as a customer, I now have an active policy. Let's say I need to file a claim. I'll provide the amount and a description. Now, let's see how the **Claims Officer** handles this."
- **Action**: Logout -> Login as **Claims Officer** -> **Claims Dashboard**.
- **What to say**: 
    > "The Claims Officer has their own specialized workflow. They see pending claims, settlement stats, and can process requests efficiently. They can review the claim details and settle it with a single click, triggering the payment process."

---

### Part 5: Admin Control Center (10:30 - 13:00)

- **Action**: Logout -> Login as **Admin** -> **Admin Dashboard**.
- **What to say**: 
    > "Finally, the **Admin Dashboard** is where the entire system is managed. Admins have a high-level overview of users, active policies, and total revenue."
- **Action**: Briefly show **Quiz Management**, **Policy Management**, and **Badge Management**.
- **What to say**: 
    > "Admins can create new quizzes, define point requirements for badges, and manage the product catalog. But most importantly, they manage the **Discount Rules**."
- **Action**: Navigate to **Discount Rules**.
- **What to say**: 
    > "This is the brain of the platform. Admins can create rules like: 'If a user has 200 points and a quiz score over 80%, give 15% off on Health Policies'. This allows the platform to be incredibly flexible and data-driven."

---

### Part 6: Conclusion (13:00 - 14:00)

- **What to say**: 
    > "IQsure is a comprehensive, end-to-end solution that transforms insurance from a complex chore into an engaging educational journey. By empowering users with knowledge and rewarding their progress, we create a win-win scenario for both the insurer and the insured. Built on a robust stack of Angular, Spring Boot, and JWT security, it's ready for the future of insurance. Thanks for watching!"
